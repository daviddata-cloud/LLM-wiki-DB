CDC Standard Operating Procedure
ID: SOP-DOC3-002
Title: Remote Work and Telework Security Standards

1.0 Purpose:
This SOP defines the security requirements and acceptable use policies for CDC employees and contractors who access CDC information systems and data while working remotely or in a telework arrangement.

2.0 Scope:
This policy applies to all CDC personnel approved for telework, including full-time remote employees, hybrid workers, and contractors accessing CDC systems from non-CDC facilities.

3.0 Device Requirements:
3.1 Approved Devices:
Only CDC-issued or formally approved devices may be used to access CDC internal systems and sensitive data. Personal devices (BYOD) are strictly prohibited for accessing systems classified as Internal, Sensitive, or Restricted.

3.2 Endpoint Security:
All approved remote devices must have the CDC-standard endpoint protection suite installed and active, including antivirus, host-based firewall, and disk encryption (BitLocker or equivalent)
